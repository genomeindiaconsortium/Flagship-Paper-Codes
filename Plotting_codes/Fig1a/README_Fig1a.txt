#This figure depicts the location of the 83 populations sampled in Genome India project. The points do not always reflect the sampling location, but rather an idealized location based on population history or current distribution or both and adjusted for clarity in visualization. 
#The shape of the points indicates whether the population is a recognized tribe (square) or not (circle), while the color indicates the ethnolinguistic group to which the population belongs.
#Supplementary Table S1.4 from the supplementary tables is used as input, as indicated in the code. 
#Background map files, provided in India_map folder, were obtained from official Survey of India website. The version used here was downloaded in December, 2024. Link - https://surveyofindia.gov.in/pages/outline-maps-of-india. 
#The coloured regions representing the distribution of language families were manually created using an external image editor.
